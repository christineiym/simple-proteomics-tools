Welcome to the amino acids to chemical formula tool! Before entering an amino acid sequence, please note the following:

  • Amino acids must be given as single letters (case insensitive). Inputs like "Arg" are currently not supported.
  • All characters that are not valid amino acids will be ignored.
  • To allow for batch processing of amino acids, new lines currently serve as the demarcation between amino acids; therefore, contiguous sequences spread across multiple lines must be adjusted accordingly for accurate results.
  • This is still very much a work in progress and will see improvements if/as the need arises.
Thank you!
